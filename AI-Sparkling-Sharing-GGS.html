HTML Report Generator Prompt

This is the prompt template Amy used to instruct the agent to transform the raw text report into an interactive consulting portal:

Export the report as a highly visual HTML dashboard with a left sidebar navigation, interactive pop-ups, visual effects, and other dynamic elements. Preserve all content from the source file without removing, shortening, or summarizing any information.

The supplier table is especially important and must remain fully intact. Every supplier listed in the report must include a clickable link directing users to that supplierâ€™s Alibaba.com storefront.

For all YoY growth figures, regulations, government policies, market statistics, and industry trends, include references and direct links to official reports, government publications, news articles, or authoritative sources.

To enrich the report, add several mini-site style showcases of leading apparel manufacturers, including images of factories, production lines, showrooms, and products. The goal is to make the HTML report visually engaging and information-rich rather than text-heavy.

Interactive pop-ups should be implemented throughout the report. When users click on a supplier, company card, chart element, or product image, a pop-up should display detailed information such as:
* Supplier profile
* Company strengths
* Certifications
* Main products
* Export markets
* MOQ and pricing information
* Product specifications
* Related market insights

Whenever possible, include direct links that take users straight to the relevant Alibaba.com storefront or product page.

The final HTML should function as an interactive market intelligence portal, combining comprehensive data, visual content, supplier information, product showcases, and authoritative references in a professional and engaging format.
