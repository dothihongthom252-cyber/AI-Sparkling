Chapter 2: Market Overview

Vietnam's bottled water market is characterized by a mix of international giants (Aquafina, La Vie) and strong local players (Vinh Hao, Vikoda). The export sector reached over $1.8M in 2024, with significant growth potential in developed markets seeking competitive sourcing [Source].

Key growth drivers include urban expansion, rising health consciousness, and a preference for "natural source" branding. Vietnamese exporters are increasingly adopting international standards such as ISO 22000 and HACCP to ensure compliance in regulated markets like the EU and USA.

Chapter 7: Strategy & Risk

Export Strategy Recommendations

1. Leverage Private Label (OEM): 60% of B2B inquiries for water involve private label requests. Offering customizable label design and bottle shapes is key. [Source]

2. Highlight Certifications: Ensure FDA (for US) and HACCP/ISO certifications are prominent in the first listing image to build instant trust. [Source]

3. Logistics Optimization: Water is heavy; shipping in 20ft/40ft containers is the only viable export model. Partnering with 4PL providers for "Door-to-Door" quotes can increase conversion.
