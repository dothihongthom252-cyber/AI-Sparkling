2026 is a breakout year for Vietnam’s bamboo and rattan handicraft sector. Chapter 5: Buyer RFQ Requests

Bulk Folding Bamboo Baskets (USA)

A U.S. buyer is looking for a factory to produce folding bamboo baskets for a retail project. Products must be eco-friendly, FSC-certified and packed efficiently to reduce shipping costs. Quarterly repeat orders are expected if the sample quality meets requirements.

Scandi Bamboo/Rattan Lampshades for the Netherlands

A Dutch importer is seeking a manufacturer for handmade modern bamboo/rattan lampshades. All electrical accessories must meet CE safety standards. Priority is given to Vietnamese factories with OEM capability based on custom technical drawings.

Fashion Woven Rattan Bags for France

A Paris fashion brand needs woven bamboo/rattan bags with high-quality canvas lining. The finish must be refined, smooth and scratch-free. Private branding tags are required, and test samples are needed quickly for a summer collection.

Rattan Resort Furniture in Australia

A premium resort project in Australia needs natural rattan furniture that can withstand coastal weather. Suppliers must provide ISPM 15 fumigation documents and have experience delivering directly to project sites in Queensland.

Premium Dining Rattan Trays (Tokyo)

A Japanese buyer requires tightly woven round rattan trays with an ultra-smooth finish. Products must meet food-contact safety requirements for use in premium restaurants in Tokyo. Strict QC is required before container loading.

Bamboo-Framed Wall Mirrors for the UK

A UK home decor retailer is looking for sunflower-style bamboo-framed mirrors. Mail-order packaging is required to prevent breakage during online sales. The quotation should include detailed air sample costs.

Bamboo/Rattan Toy Storage Baskets (Canada)

The buyer is looking for lidded rattan baskets for children with no harmful chemicals used in raw-material processing. The product targets the premium mother-and-baby segment in North America and requires Canadian toy-safety compliance.

Premium Sedge Mats for Villas in Dubai

A Dubai client needs large 2m x 3m sedge mats for a villa project. The mats must use carefully selected natural sedge, be fully handwoven, and have strong anti-mold treatment for air-conditioned environments. Roll packing with end protection is required for sea freight.

Zen-Style Bamboo/Rattan Floor Lamps (Seoul)

A buyer is seeking a partner to produce bamboo/rattan floor lamps for a Zen-style cafe chain in Seoul. The frame must be sturdy, with fine weaving that creates artistic shadows. Cables and plugs must meet Korean 220V standards and electrical safety requirements.

Compact Mini Rattan Dining Set (Singapore)

A Singapore buyer is looking for a compact rattan dining set for modern apartments. Lightweight yet durable products are preferred. The supplier should support LCL consolidation and delivery to the buyer’s designated warehouse at Jurong Port.

CHAPTER 8

Chapter 8: Strategy & Research Notes

The 2026 strategy should focus on higher value + certification + custom design, instead of competing only on price in basic baskets. Vietnam has advantages in refined hand-weaving, craft-village heritage, flexible OEM capability and natural-material storytelling.

01

Prioritize higher-margin categories

Focus on bamboo/rattan lamps, wall decor, trays/gift baskets and fashion rattan bags. These categories can be differentiated through shape, fiber color, weaving pattern, seasonal collections and manageable MOQs.

02

Strengthen certification & C/O

For buyers in the EU, Japan and the U.S., sellers should prepare C/O, material certificates, chemical-control records, packaging processes and traceability files. These help build trust and reduce price pressure.

03

Package for e-commerce

Design packaging to prevent deformation and moisture damage, optimize container space and support online sales. For lamps, mirrors and small furniture, packaging directly affects return rates and buyer reviews.

04

Build minisites by buyer scenario

Do not list products only as isolated items. Organize them by scenarios such as Hotel & Resort, Home Decor Retail, Amazon/Wayfair Seller, Eco Gift Set and Fashion Summer Collection so buyers can understand application value.

05

Tiered pricing strategy

Use basic baskets/trays to attract inquiry volume, then upsell to lampshades, gift sets, decor sets and OEM collections to increase AOV. Pricing should clearly separate sample price, FOB bulk price and custom design fee.

06

Sell through visual content

Images should include lifestyle scenes, size comparisons, close-ups of weaving, packaging photos, container/loading shots and craft-village process videos. Handicrafts need to sell through visual trust.

Conclusion: Vietnam should position itself around “premium handmade + sustainable material + OEM design”. The highest-priority product group is rattan/bamboo lampshades at around $4.50 - $15.10/pcs, then expand into storage baskets/gift baskets at around $2.40 - $9.68/pcs to maintain both inquiry volume and profit margin.
